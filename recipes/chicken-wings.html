Chicken Wings

YUM!

This dish is a favorite at my household! I first learned this recipe from my lovely girlfriend. It's been a family secret for years, but Im excited to share it with you all!

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs of Bone-In Chicken Wings
  • 1 cup of Ghee
  • 2 cups of Louisiana Hot Sauce
  • 3 tbsp of Black Pepper
