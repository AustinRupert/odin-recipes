Chicken Wings

Fall off the bone wings that have a kick to them!

This dish is a favorite at my household! I first learned this recipe from my lovely girlfriend.

It's been a family secret for years, but Im excited to share it with you all!

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs of Bone-In Chicken Wings
  • 1 cup of Ghee
  • 2 cups of Louisiana Hot Sauce
  • 3 tbsp of Black Pepper

Instructions

Pro-tip: Use less sauce to make them less spicy

  1. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Lay wings out neatly on a casserole dish
  3. Mix 1/2 cup of Ghee, 1 1/2 tbsp of pepper, & 1 cup of hot sauce in a small pot over medium heat
  4. Once the sauce is simmering, pour it on the wings evenly
  5. Cover the casserole dish in aluminum foil and bake in the oven for 1 hour
  6. Cook a new pot of sauce, then take the wings out of the oven
  7. Pour sauce on wings, cover with foil again and then place wings back in oven for 40 minutes
  8. Take wings out of the oven and let rest for 10-15 minutes
  9. Make sure to have something cold to drink & enjoy!
