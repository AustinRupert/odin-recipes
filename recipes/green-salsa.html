Mexican Avocado Salsa

A creamy salsa that goes GREAT with chips!

This is my FAVORITE salsa of all time. It compliments everything from tacos to a salad dressing!

It's is one of my Mom's specialties, so I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 Serrano Peppers
  • Handful of Cilantro
  • 1 Lime
  • 1/4 Milk

Instructions

  1. Slice up the avocado and throw it in the blender along with the peppers,cilantro, and milk

    2. Careful! This next step is super important!

  2. While on a slower blending speed, slowly pour about a 1/3 cup of water into the blender
  3. Salt to taste and serve!
