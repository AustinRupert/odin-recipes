Tostadas

A childhood favorite that is super easy to make!

Tostadas are one of my favorite dishes of all time. They're easy to make and are delicious!

Be sure to try them with the Mexican Avocado Salsa

Ingredients

  • Pack of Tostadas (I recommend these)
  • Refried beans
  • Avocado
  • Queso Fresco Crumbles
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Sliced Tomato
  • (Optional) Protein of choice
  • Mexican Crema

Instructions

  1. Take a tostada and spread the refried beans on it. This is your base
  2. Now add your lettuce, protein, tomato, and avocado
  3. Sprinkle the queso fresco generously on the tostada
  4. Add salsa and/or crema and enjoy!
